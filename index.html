Logging Rules

  • Only beers count. Wine and other beverages cannot be logged.
  • Log your beer right after you finish it. If you forget to log a beer on time, don't log it at all.
  • Beers drunk in moving vehicles (trains, buses, cars) are not allowed to be logged.
  • Each beer is worth exactly 1 token, regardless of size or alcohol content.

How it Works

  • Most tokens in a Gemeinde 🚩 claims it.
  • Claim the most area in a Canton 🏆 to conquer it.
  • Each conquered Canton is worth one point on the Leaderboard 🥇.

Location Required 📍

  1. Open your iPhone Settings app.
  2. Scroll down and tap Privacy & Security.
  3. Tap Location Services and ensure it's turned ON.
  4. Scroll down the app list to your browser (e.g., Safari or Chrome).
  5. Change permission to While Using the App.
  6. Return here and reload the page!
