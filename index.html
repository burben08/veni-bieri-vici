The Goal: Conquer as much of Switzerland as possible by claiming municipalities (Gemeinden) and entire Cantons.

How to Play:

  • Drink a beer and tap a Gemeinde to place a token 🍺.
  • 📸 Every time you log a beer, you must send a selfie with the beer in the WhatsApp group!
  • Important: Non-alcoholic beers are fully allowed!
  • Important: All sizes (3dl, 5dl, etc.) are worth exactly ONE token.
  • Have the most tokens in a Gemeinde to claim it 🚩.
  • Control the largest area within a Canton to conquer the entire Canton 🏆.

Keep an eye on the Leaderboard and watch out for other players taking your territory!
